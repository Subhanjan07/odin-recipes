Pasta Recipe

Description

Pasta is an Italian staple dish that comes in various forms and styles. It's often served with a variety of sauces, with the most common being tomato sauce, Alfredo, and pesto. This recipe will guide you through making a classic tomato pasta.

Ingredients

  • For Pasta
    • 250g Penne Pasta
    • Salt
    • Water
  • For Sauce
    • Tomato Sauce
    • Olive Oil
    • Garlic
    • Onion
    • Oregano
    • Fresh Basil

Steps:

  1. Cook the pasta
    1. Boil a pot of water with a pinch of salt.
    2. Add the penne pasta and cook according to package instructions, usually around 10 minutes.
    3. Drain the pasta and set aside.
  2. Make the tomato sauce
    1. Heat olive oil in a pan, and saut√© chopped garlic and onions until golden brown.
    2. Add tomato sauce, oregano, and simmer for about 10 minutes.
    3. Once the sauce thickens, stir in fresh basil.
  3. Combine pasta and sauce
    1. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and mix well to coat the pasta evenly.
  4. Serve hot, optionally garnishing with Parmesan cheese and extra basil.
