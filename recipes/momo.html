Momo Recipe

Description

Momo is a Nepali dish which is similar to a dumpling in looks but the taste is very different. The differentiating factor is the fillings and the dipping sauce. Let's dive deeper into the recipe of making momos.

Ingrediants

  • For Dough
    • Flour
    • Salt
    • Lukewarm Water
  • For Fillings
    • Chicken Mince
    • Onions
    • Celery
    • Ginger
    • Pepper

Steps:

  1. For Dough
    1. Mix the flour, salt and lukewarm water properly and knead the mixture.
    2. Once you finish mixing the mixture, cover the dough and let it rest for about 30 mins.
    3. Make small portions of about 5-7 grams and make small balls from each portion.
  2. Chicken Fillings
    1. Mix all of the ingrediants for the chicken fillings. And the fillings will cook when we steam the momo.
  3. We then roll out the portioned dough, add the fillings and fold the momos to close the opening.
  4. Add a little bit of oil throughout the steamer and place the momos in the steamer.
  5. Steam the momos for 6-10 minutes, and your delicious homemade momo is ready to eat.
