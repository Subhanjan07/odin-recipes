Chicken Curry Recipe

Description

Chicken curry is a popular dish made with chicken cooked in a rich, flavorful sauce made of spices, herbs, and a creamy base. It is perfect for serving with rice or naan.

Ingredients

  • For Curry
    • 500g Chicken (boneless, cut into pieces)
    • 2 Onions (finely chopped)
    • 2 Tomatoes (pureed)
    • 2 tsp Ginger-Garlic paste
    • 1 tsp Cumin seeds
    • 1 tsp Turmeric powder
    • 1 tsp Red chili powder
    • 1 tsp Garam masala
    • Salt to taste
    • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)
    • Cooking oil

Steps:

  1. Prepare the ingredients
    1. Chop the onions, tomatoes, and chicken pieces.
  2. Cooking the curry
    1. Heat oil in a large pan, add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
    2. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
    3. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
    4. Add pureed tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Let the mixture cook for 5 minutes.
    5. Add chicken pieces and stir to coat them in the spices. Cook for 10 minutes.
    6. Pour in water to cover the chicken, bring it to a boil, then simmer for 20 minutes until the chicken is fully cooked.
  3. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve with rice or naan.
